What is this website about?

I'm glad you asked. This website is basically a Résumé, but with a lot more details and personal thoughts than your regular CV.
I also talk more in-depth about the different projects I have worked on, and what the taught me.
I was also thinking about writing some blog posts about game development and computer graphics. But I don't believe it has its place in a Github website.

If you want to have an idea on what I've done so far, you can have a look at the projects page where I talk in detail about all the games I've worked on. You can also have a look at my résumé which is basically a condensed version of this portfolio.

I am currently working at a company called Virtuos, well known in the game industry for providing various art assets to AAA games and movies. But also for making remasters and portings of great titles such as : L.A. Noire VR, The Ezio collection and Batman: Return to Arkham.

In 2015, I graduated with a DUT (french equivalent of a HNC) in computer science. During my studies, I did a 6 months work placement in a video game company called Polm Studio (I know it doesn't look like it, but they used to make games), working on a mobile game called Maya Wars on Android and iOS.

During my free time, I love learning about computer graphics and programming in general. I even like making shaders for fun. You can have a look on my creations on shadertoy. I am working on my own little game called Wolfen-Doo, a mix between Wolfenstein 3D and Scooby-doo. I also like challenging myself with programming problems and games.

Like most people in the game industry, I like playing games. My favourites include the Monkey Island series and the Grand Theft Auto series. I also like watching movies (big fan of Scorsese), coding for fun, and sometimes I even go outside on a walk. I also very recently started playing american football.
